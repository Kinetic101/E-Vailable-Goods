1. kung may antivirus, pa off nung firewall ng antivirus
2. open "Windows Defender Firewall with Advanced Security", search niyo lang sa win10
3. click inbound rules sa left side
4. click new rule sa right side
5. select port then next
6. select TCP then select specific local ports then type 80 dun sa input bar then next
7. select allow the connection then next
8. select all then next
9. kayo bahala kung anong ipangalan niyo then finish
10. i copy paste niyo yung httpd-vhosts.conf sa Administrator folder papunta sa C:\wamp64\bin\apache\apache2.4.46\conf\extra, i replace file
11. sa device except sa laptop na ginamit para isetup yung preceeding rules, itype sa browser yung ip address ng laptop na naglalaman nung evailable goods website then /E-Vailable-Goods/User
    since tigconfigure ko para di maaccess ng client yung buong E-Vailable Goods folder for security, hanapin niyo na langs yung Login.php or SignUp.php
    e.g. 192.168.0.101//E-Vailable-Goods/User
12. wag kalimutan i on yung firewall tas idisable yung rule

*para malaman yung ip in case di kayo marunong, type ipconfig sa cmd sa laptop na may wamp tas hanapin niyo yung IPv4 Address na may value
*accessible yung site sa lahat ng devices
